Indian Singers

Shreya Ghoshal

Shreya Ghoshal is one of India’s most iconic and versatile playback singers. She began her musical journey at a young age and gained fame after winning the reality show Sa Re Ga Ma Pa. She made her Bollywood debut with Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s Devdas in 2002, earning a National Award at just 18.
